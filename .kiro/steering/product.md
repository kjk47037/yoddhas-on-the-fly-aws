# Product Overview

## Product Name
OnTheFly - AI-Powered Social Media Marketing Platform

## Purpose
OnTheFly is an intelligent marketing automation platform that helps small businesses and content creators generate, optimize, and manage social media content across multiple platforms using AI and machine learning.

## Target Users
- Small business owners
- Solo entrepreneurs
- Content creators
- Social media managers at startups
- Marketing teams with limited resources

## Key Features

### Content Studio
AI-powered content generation with platform-specific optimization for Instagram, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn. Includes real-time ML predictions for engagement performance.

### Brand Kit Generator
Automated brand identity creation including logos, color palettes, typography recommendations, and brand guidelines.

### Campaign Builder
Multi-platform campaign management with scheduling, audience targeting, and performance tracking.

### Analytics Dashboard
Real-time analytics aggregation from connected social platforms with AI-generated insights and recommendations.

### SEO Audit
Website analysis for search engine optimization with actionable recommendations.

### Competitor Analysis
Instagram competitor analysis with AI-powered insights using Gemini API.

## Business Objectives
1. Reduce content creation time by 80%
2. Improve social media engagement through ML-optimized content
3. Provide enterprise-level marketing tools at affordable pricing
4. Enable data-driven decision making for small businesses

## Value Proposition
"Create better content, faster. Our AI learns what works for your audience and helps you replicate success."
